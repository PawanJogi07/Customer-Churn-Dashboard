# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into customer retention, churn trends, revenue impact, contract distribution, and state-wise customer performance.

## 🖼 Dashboard Preview

![Dashboard Screenshot](images/dashboard.png)

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer churn directly affects business growth and profitability. This dashboard helps identify:

- Churned vs Active Customers
- Revenue contribution by customer segments
- Contract-wise customer distribution
- State-wise revenue performance
- Customer tenure trends

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

### 1. KPI Cards
Provides high-level business metrics:
- Total Customers
- Total Revenue
- Active Customers
- Churn Rate
- Churned Customers

### 2. Contract Analysis
Bar chart showing customer distribution by:
- Month-to-Month
- One Year
- Two Year

### 3. Customer Status Distribution
Pie chart displaying:
- Active Customers
- Joined Customers
- Churned Customers

### 4. Customer Tenure Trend
Line chart analyzing:
- Active Customers over tenure
- Churned Customers over tenure
- Newly Joined Customers over tenure

### 5. State-wise Revenue Analysis
Stacked bar chart showing revenue contribution by:
- Customer Status
- State

## 🚀 Insights

- Majority customers are under Month-to-Month contracts.
- Churn rate is approximately 26%.

- Certain states contribute significantly more revenue than others.
- Customer tenure has a strong relationship with churn behavior.
